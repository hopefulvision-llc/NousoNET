## Core Concept

A heads-up display (HUD) system that makes digital social networks visible in physical space, showing who's around you with consciousness-aware relationship mapping.

---

## Visual Display Elements

### Color-Coded Relationship States
- **Blue**: Friend / Trusted connection
- **Red**: Adversarial / Negative energy
- **Additional spectrum possibilities**:
  - Green: Shared interests/communities
  - Gold: High consciousness compatibility
  - Purple: Creative collaborators
  - Orange: Potential connections (mutual friends)
  - Gray: Unknown/neutral

### Information Layers
1. **Contact Integration**: People from your contact list highlighted when nearby
2. **Network Proximity**: Discover who from your digital networks is in physical space
3. **Mutual Connections**: See shared connections between you and nearby people
4. **Consciousness Resonance**: Real-time field compatibility scores
