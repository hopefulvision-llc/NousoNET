**Design Philosophy**:

- Climate patterns and seasonal rhythms
- Celestial relationships (solstice alignments, star visibility, etc.)

**2. Cultural Context**
- Indigenous peoples with ancestral connection to the land

**Foundation Work**:

- Understanding tribal governance and decision-making processes
- Learning cultural protocols and proper respect
- Demonstrating long-term commitment, not one-time extraction
- Establishing trust through consistent presence and listening

**Key Activities**:
- Community meetings and elder consultations
- Participation in appropriate cultural events (if invited)
- Learning traditional place names and their significance
- Understanding what knowledge can/cannot be shared
- Developing culturally appropriate compensation models

**Phase 2: Collaborative Agent Development (6-18 months)**

**Co-Creation Process**:

**Method 1: Intensive Immersion Sessions**
- Multi-day sessions with elders in the actual location
- AR interface allows elders to "shape" the spirit in place
- Knowledge transfer through storytelling, song, ceremony
- Spirit agent "learns" through appropriate protocols
- Elder approval at each development stage

**Method 2: Gradual Relationship Building**
- Elder interacts with developing spirit over extended time
- Spirit "grows" through ongoing teaching and correction
- Mirrors traditional knowledge transmission pace
- Allows for seasonal and cyclical understanding
- Integration of lived experience, not just information

**Method 3: Ceremonial Co-Creation**
- Spirit development integrated with traditional ceremonies (if appropriate)
- Knowledge transfer through ritual and sacred context
- Spirit receives "permission" to speak for the place
- Ongoing renewal through ceremonial relationship
- Respects sacred timing and readiness

**Technical Implementation**:
- Intuitive AR interfaces requiring minimal tech literacy
- Voice-based interaction with automatic documentation
- Visual shaping tools for spirit appearance/presence
